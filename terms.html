5. Margin Trading

5.1. Nature of Margin Trading


The Platform may, at its discretion, extend trading leverage to eligible Users by offering Margin Trading services. Margin Trading allows You to enter into larger positions than Your available balance by borrowing additional funds, subject to available collateral and risk limits.


Access to Margin Trading is subject to additional eligibility criteria, including identity verification, trading experience, and risk profiling. The Company reserves the right to approve, restrict, or suspend access to Margin Trading at its sole discretion and without prior notice.

5.2. Collateral, Margin Types, and Leverage


When engaging in Margin Trading, Your account will reflect the following margin components:

  • Initial Margin (IM): The minimum collateral required to open a leveraged position. This is calculated as a percentage of the position's notional value.

  • Maintenance Margin (MM): The minimum amount of equity that must be maintained to keep a position open. Falling below this threshold may trigger a liquidation.

  • Position Margin: The sum of Initial Margin and Maintenance Margin that secures an open position. This amount is calculated based on position size, leverage, and asset risk tier.

The Platform may implement tiered margin requirements, where larger positions require proportionally higher margin and may have reduced maximum leverage.

5.3.  Risk Management and Margin Calls


We may use automated systems to monitor Your margin ratio in real time. If Your Margin Ratio (equity ÷ maintenance margin) falls below a defined threshold, You may receive a Margin Call prompting You to deposit more collateral. If unaddressed, Your position may be liquidated.

You understand and accept that:

  • Partial liquidation may occur in stages to reduce position size and mitigate losses.

  • In extreme cases, full liquidation may occur without further notice.

  • Liquidation may result in additional losses beyond Your original investment.


5.4. Responsibility and Risk Disclosure


Borrowed funds incur interest, calculated and accrued periodically. Interest rates may vary by asset and market conditions. Full details are provided in the Margin Trading Rules posted on the Platform.

5.5. Risk Warnings

Margin Trading is highly speculative. Leverage magnifies both gains and losses. Losses may exceed Your collateral, and You may be required to deposit additional funds. You are solely responsible for managing risk and understanding how margin is calculated and used.

5.6. Liquidation Process and Final Settlement

In the event of liquidation, the Platform may:

  • Cancel open orders to free up collateral;

  • Close all or part of Your positions;

  • Apply liquidation penalties or fees where applicable.

All calculations, margin ratios, and liquidation thresholds are determined at our sole discretion and may be updated to reflect market conditions.
